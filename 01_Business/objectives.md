# Objectives

## 1. Document Purpose

This document defines the measurable business and product objectives of **StackLeo Tech Store**. It converts the vision defined in `vision.md` and the mission defined in `mission.md` into clear, actionable, and measurable objectives that guide planning, execution, and performance measurement.

Where the vision describes what StackLeo aims to become and the mission describes how it operates day to day, this document defines what StackLeo intends to achieve, and how progress toward those achievements will be recognized. Objectives in this document are structured, where appropriate, using SMART principles — Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

This document does not describe implementation approach, technology choices, system architecture, or database design, all of which are addressed in dedicated technical documentation elsewhere in the repository. Exact figures, financial targets, and delivery dates referenced here are directional and subject to confirmation through dedicated business and financial planning.

## 2. Objectives Overview

Objectives are organized across the dimensions most relevant to StackLeo Tech Store's current and future direction:

- **Strategic Objectives** — the overarching aims that connect vision to execution.
- **Business, Sales, and Financial Objectives** — what the company aims to achieve commercially.
- **Customer and Customer Service Objectives** — what the company aims to deliver for the people it serves.
- **Product, Operational, and Technology Objectives** — what the platform and business must achieve functionally and operationally.
- **Marketing, Scalability, and Sustainability Objectives** — how the company aims to grow responsibly over time.

Each objective category below is intended to be reviewed and refined as the project progresses from its current Planning & Documentation stage into execution.

## 3. Strategic Objectives

| Objective | Description | Target Timeframe |
|---|---|---|
| Establish market credibility | Position StackLeo Tech Store as a recognized, trustworthy technology marketplace in Bangladesh. | Within the first year of operation. |
| Validate the single-seller B2C model | Confirm the viability of the current business model before pursuing expansion. | Within the MVP launch period. |
| Prepare for future business models | Establish a business and platform foundation capable of supporting B2B, corporate sales, and marketplace expansion. | Ongoing, from MVP stage onward. |

## 4. Business Objectives

- Establish StackLeo as a dependable, trusted brand within Bangladesh's technology e-commerce industry.
- Build a sustainable, profitable single-seller B2C business as the foundation for future growth.
- Achieve consistent, reliable operations across both the online store and physical retail store.
- Lay the groundwork for corporate sales, wholesale, and multi-vendor marketplace expansion, consistent with `business-model.md`.

## 5. Customer Objectives

| Objective | Measurable Target |
|---|---|
| Build customer trust | Achieve a consistently positive customer satisfaction rating across both sales channels. |
| Encourage repeat purchasing | Achieve a defined, growing repeat purchase rate within the first year of operation. |
| Ensure consistent channel experience | Maintain equivalent pricing, catalog, and service quality across the online store and physical retail store at all times. |
| Improve customer confidence in authenticity | Maintain a low, clearly tracked rate of product authenticity or warranty-related disputes. |

## 6. Sales Objectives

| Objective | Measurable Target |
|---|---|
| Grow overall sales volume | Achieve steady, quarter-over-quarter growth in total units sold across both channels. |
| Grow customer base | Achieve continuous growth in the number of active customers following MVP launch. |
| Establish physical retail as a viable channel | Achieve a defined, sustainable contribution of in-store sales to total revenue. |
| Prepare for corporate sales | Identify and document initial corporate sales opportunities ahead of future channel launch. |

## 7. Product Objectives

- Maintain a broad, well-organized catalog across all core product categories defined in `business-requirements.md`.
- Ensure product availability remains consistent and accurately reflected across both sales channels.
- Continuously improve product discovery and catalog organization based on customer feedback.
- Expand catalog breadth over time in line with validated customer demand.

## 8. Operational Objectives

| Objective | Measurable Target |
|---|---|
| Maintain order fulfillment accuracy | Achieve a consistently high order accuracy rate across both online and in-store fulfillment. |
| Maintain delivery reliability | Achieve dependable, on-time delivery performance for online orders. |
| Maintain inventory accuracy | Achieve consistent, accurate stock visibility across both sales channels. |
| Support operational scalability | Ensure operational processes can absorb steady growth in order volume without service degradation. |

## 9. Technology Objectives (High-Level Only)

- Ensure the platform remains reliable and consistently available to customers across sales channels.
- Ensure the platform's foundation can evolve to support future channels, including a mobile app, marketplace, corporate portal, and wholesale portal.
- Ensure technical decisions support, rather than constrain, the business and customer objectives defined in this document.

Detailed technical architecture, technology stack, and implementation approach are outside the scope of this document and are addressed in dedicated technical documentation.

## 10. Marketing Objectives

| Objective | Measurable Target |
|---|---|
| Build brand awareness | Achieve measurable growth in brand recognition within the primary market. |
| Drive customer acquisition | Achieve a steady, sustainable rate of new customer acquisition following launch. |
| Reinforce trust-based positioning | Ensure marketing communications consistently reflect StackLeo's commitment to authenticity and reliability. |
| Support channel awareness | Ensure customers are aware of and can easily access both the online store and physical retail store. |

## 11. Financial Objectives

- Achieve positive, sustainable revenue growth across current revenue streams, as defined in `business-model.md`.
- Maintain a cost structure proportionate to the business's current single-seller B2C stage.
- Establish a clear path toward profitability appropriate to the MVP stage before pursuing broader expansion.
- Diversify revenue over time through future streams, including corporate sales, wholesale, extended warranty, and marketplace commission.

Detailed financial targets and figures are managed through dedicated financial planning, separate from this document.

## 12. Customer Service Objectives

| Objective | Measurable Target |
|---|---|
| Maintain responsive support | Achieve a consistently fast, defined response time for customer inquiries. |
| Resolve issues effectively | Achieve a high first-contact resolution rate for customer support inquiries. |
| Ensure fair warranty and returns handling | Maintain consistent adherence to the terms defined in `warranty-policy.md` and `return-policy.md`. |
| Improve support based on feedback | Continuously incorporate customer feedback into support process improvements. |

## 13. Scalability Objectives

- Ensure business processes, inventory management, and fulfillment can scale to support growing order volume without compromising reliability.
- Ensure the platform and operating model can extend to new channels, including a mobile app, marketplace, corporate portal, and wholesale portal, without requiring a fundamental redesign.
- Validate scalability incrementally, in line with the phased growth strategy defined in `business-model.md`.

## 14. Sustainability Objectives

- Grow the business at a pace that preserves operational stability and customer trust.
- Maintain responsible sourcing and partnership practices as the business scales.
- Avoid pursuing new channels or business models before the current stage has been validated.
- Ensure long-term growth remains consistent with the disciplined philosophy defined in `mission.md`.

## 15. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI Category | Example Metric |
|---|---|
| Customer Growth | Growth in total active customers across both sales channels. |
| Customer Trust | Customer satisfaction rating and repeat purchase rate. |
| Sales Performance | Total sales volume and revenue across current revenue streams. |
| Channel Performance | Relative and combined performance of the online store and physical retail store. |
| Operational Reliability | Order accuracy, delivery timeliness, and inventory accuracy. |
| Support Quality | Response time and resolution rate for customer service inquiries. |
| Financial Health | Revenue growth and cost proportionality relative to business stage. |
| Expansion Readiness | Progress toward readiness for corporate sales, wholesale, and marketplace capabilities. |

Detailed targets, thresholds, and reporting cadence for these KPIs will be defined through dedicated business planning and performance management processes.

## 16. Objective Review Process

Objectives are expected to evolve as the project progresses from planning through MVP launch and future expansion. The following principles guide this process:

- Objectives should be reviewed at the conclusion of each major phase defined in `00_Project_Overview/project-roadmap.md`.
- Any change to an objective, including its addition, removal, or modification, must be reflected by updating this document and incrementing its version number.
- All changes to objectives must be recorded in `00_Project_Overview/changelog.md`, including the reasoning behind the change.
- Objective reviews should confirm continued alignment with `vision.md`, `mission.md`, and `business-model.md`.
- Objectives that are achieved, superseded, or no longer relevant should be clearly marked and replaced with updated objectives appropriate to the business's current stage.
