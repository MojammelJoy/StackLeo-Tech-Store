# 01_Business

## 1. Folder Overview

This folder contains the business documentation for the **StackLeo Tech Store** project. It defines the business foundations that guide the platform's direction — including its business model, vision, mission, objectives, market positioning, and the policies that govern how StackLeo operates and serves its customers.

This README describes the contents of this folder only. It is not the project's main README and does not describe the repository as a whole.

## 2. Purpose

The `01_Business` folder translates the foundational understanding established in `00_Project_Overview` into concrete business definitions, rules, and policies. It ensures that business analysts, product managers, designers, developers, QA engineers, and stakeholders share a consistent understanding of how StackLeo Tech Store operates as a business, before functional and technical documentation is introduced elsewhere in the repository.

## 3. Business Documentation Objectives

The documents in this folder are intended to:

- Define the formal business requirements that guide the project's direction.
- Clarify the business model and how StackLeo Tech Store generates and sustains value.
- Establish the vision, mission, and objectives that guide business decision-making.
- Describe the target market and the competitive landscape StackLeo Tech Store operates within.
- Provide a structured assessment of the business's strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats.
- Define the business rules that govern platform operations.
- Establish policies covering pricing, shipping, returns, and warranty to ensure consistent customer treatment.
- Provide a centralized reference for frequently asked business-related questions.

## 7. Documentation Guidelines

- Keep documents concise, factual, and free of implementation-level detail.
- Use clear headings and consistent formatting across all documents in this folder.
- Update the relevant document promptly when business understanding, policy, or direction changes, and log the change in `00_Project_Overview/changelog.md`.
- Avoid duplicating content across documents; reference related documents instead of repeating information.
- Ensure business policies documented here remain consistent with the scope and goals defined in `00_Project_Overview`.
- Write for a mixed audience — avoid unexplained jargon and align terminology with `00_Project_Overview/glossary.md`.
- Keep this README aligned with the actual contents of the folder whenever documents are added, renamed, or removed.
