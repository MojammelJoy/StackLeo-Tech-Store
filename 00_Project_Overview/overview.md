# Project Overview

## 1. Project Overview

**StackLeo Tech Store** is a technology and electronics e-commerce platform being developed by **StackLeo** to serve the Bangladesh market. The platform is positioned as a single, reliable marketplace for technology products — reflected in its tagline, *"Everything Tech, One Marketplace."*

This document introduces the project at a business and product level. It defines what StackLeo Tech Store is, why it is being built, who it serves, and the vision guiding its long-term direction. Functional, technical, and architectural details are documented separately elsewhere in the repository.

## 2. About StackLeo

StackLeo is the company behind StackLeo Tech Store. It operates in the technology and electronics e-commerce industry, with an initial focus on building a dependable online marketplace tailored to the needs of customers in Bangladesh.

## 3. About StackLeo Tech Store

StackLeo Tech Store is StackLeo's flagship product — an e-commerce marketplace dedicated to technology and electronics. It is designed to bring a broad range of tech products together under one platform, giving customers a consistent and trustworthy place to discover and purchase the products they need.

## 4. Vision

To become the most trusted and preferred technology marketplace in Bangladesh, and a recognized name in the region's technology e-commerce industry.

## 5. Mission

To provide customers with reliable access to genuine technology products through a simple, trustworthy, and customer-focused online marketplace.

## 6. Business Objectives

- Establish StackLeo as a credible brand within the technology e-commerce industry.
- Build sustainable customer trust through reliability and consistency.
- Create a scalable business model that supports long-term growth.
- Expand market reach within Bangladesh before pursuing regional growth.
- Lay the groundwork for future business models, including B2B and multi-vendor marketplace operations.

## 7. Project Objectives

- Define a clear and well-documented foundation for the StackLeo Tech Store platform.
- Establish the product's scope, goals, and stakeholders before development begins.
- Ensure all planning and documentation support a customer-focused, technology-focused marketplace.
- Create a structure that allows the project to evolve without losing clarity or direction.

## 8. Target Audience

| Audience | Description |
|---|---|
| Individual Consumers | Customers purchasing technology and electronics products for personal use. |
| Tech Enthusiasts | Customers seeking a wide range of tech products in one marketplace. |
| Small Businesses (Future) | Business customers expected to be served as the platform expands toward B2B capabilities. |
| Future Marketplace Sellers | Vendors expected to join the platform once multi-vendor marketplace capabilities are introduced. |

## 9. Product Categories

StackLeo Tech Store is intended to offer a broad range of technology and electronics product categories, including but not limited to:

- Computers and laptops
- Mobile phones and accessories
- Audio and wearable devices
- Networking and smart home devices
- Gaming and entertainment devices
- General electronics and accessories

The specific catalog structure will be defined in dedicated product and catalog documentation.

## 10. High-Level Core Features

- A centralized marketplace for technology and electronics products.
- Organized product categories to support easy discovery.
- A customer-focused shopping experience built on trust and reliability.
- A foundation designed to support future business expansion, including multi-vendor selling.

Detailed functional requirements are documented separately.

## 11. Business Model

StackLeo Tech Store currently operates as a **Business-to-Consumer (B2C)** platform, selling technology and electronics products directly to individual customers.

The business model is designed to be future-ready, allowing evolution toward:

- **Business-to-Business (B2B)** sales for organizational and bulk customers.
- **Multi-vendor marketplace** operations, enabling third-party sellers to list products on the platform.

## 12. Project Scope Summary

StackLeo Tech Store, at its current stage, is focused on establishing the project's foundational documentation and planning. This includes defining the product concept, business direction, stakeholders, and high-level objectives.

Detailed scope boundaries — including what is explicitly included and excluded from the project — are defined in `project-scope.md`.

## 13. Long-Term Vision

Over time, StackLeo Tech Store is intended to grow beyond a single-seller B2C platform into a broader technology marketplace, supporting multiple vendors and business customers. The long-term direction favors sustainable, trust-driven growth within Bangladesh before expanding into a wider regional presence.
