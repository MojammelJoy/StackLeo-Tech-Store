# Project Goals

## 1. Document Purpose

This document defines the strategic goals of the **StackLeo Tech Store** project. It explains what the project intends to achieve from business, customer, operational, and technical perspectives, and establishes the outcomes the project will be measured against as it progresses.

This document is intended to guide planning and decision-making throughout the project lifecycle. Where a decision affects direction, priority, or trade-offs, it should be evaluated against the goals defined here.

This document does not describe scope boundaries, which are defined in `project-scope.md`, nor does it describe implementation approach, technology choices, or system design, which are addressed in dedicated technical documentation elsewhere in the repository.

## 2. Project Goals Overview

The goals of StackLeo Tech Store are organized across several perspectives, each representing a different dimension of what the project must achieve to succeed:

- **Business Goals** — what the company aims to achieve commercially.
- **Customer Experience Goals** — what customers should experience when using the platform.
- **Product Goals** — what the platform itself is expected to deliver.
- **Operational Goals** — how the business intends to run the platform reliably.
- **Technical Goals** — the high-level qualities the platform must exhibit, without prescribing implementation.
- **Security, Scalability, and Performance Goals** — the foundational qualities required to support trust and growth.
- **Long-Term Goals** — the direction the project is expected to grow toward over time.

Together, these goals form a shared reference point for prioritization and evaluation across all teams contributing to the project.

## 3. Business Goals

- Establish StackLeo as a credible and trustworthy brand within the technology e-commerce industry in Bangladesh.
- Build a sustainable, profitable business model centered on direct-to-consumer technology retail.
- Develop a loyal customer base through consistent reliability and genuine product offerings.
- Create a scalable business foundation capable of supporting future expansion into B2B sales and multi-vendor marketplace operations.
- Position StackLeo Tech Store as a recognized name in Bangladesh's technology e-commerce market before pursuing regional growth.

## 4. Customer Experience Goals

- Provide customers with a simple, intuitive, and trustworthy shopping experience.
- Ensure customers can easily discover and compare technology products across well-organized categories.
- Build customer confidence through consistency, transparency, and genuine product assurance.
- Minimize friction throughout the customer journey, from product discovery to order completion.
- Ensure customers feel that StackLeo Tech Store is a dependable single destination for their technology needs, in line with the tagline *"Everything Tech, One Marketplace."*

## 5. Product Goals

- Deliver a centralized marketplace that brings together a broad range of technology and electronics products.
- Maintain a well-organized product catalog that supports easy navigation and discovery.
- Ensure the platform reflects a consistent, professional brand experience across the customer journey.
- Build the product on a foundation flexible enough to support future business models without requiring a redefinition of the core platform.
- Continuously align product direction with validated customer needs rather than assumptions.

## 6. Operational Goals

- Establish clear, well-documented foundations for planning, scope, and decision-making before development begins.
- Ensure the business can reliably fulfill customer orders and maintain consistent service quality.
- Build operational processes that support accountability and clarity across teams and stakeholders.
- Maintain documentation and governance practices that keep the project coherent as it grows.
- Ensure operational readiness is validated at the MVP stage before pursuing further expansion.

## 7. Technical Goals (High-Level Only)

- Ensure the platform is reliable and consistently available to customers.
- Ensure the platform is built on a foundation capable of evolving to support future business models and capabilities.
- Ensure the platform can be maintained and extended over time without compromising stability.
- Ensure technical decisions support, rather than constrain, the business and customer experience goals defined in this document.

Detailed technical architecture, technology stack, and implementation approach are outside the scope of this document and are addressed in dedicated technical documentation.

## 8. Security Goals

- Protect customer trust by safeguarding customer and business data appropriately.
- Ensure the platform supports safe and trustworthy transactions.
- Build security considerations into planning from the earliest stages of the project, rather than addressing them only after issues arise.
- Maintain a security posture appropriate to a growing e-commerce platform handling customer information and transactions.

## 9. Scalability Goals

- Design the business and platform foundation to accommodate growth in customer base, product catalog, and order volume.
- Ensure the platform can evolve to support future business models, including B2B sales and multi-vendor marketplace operations, without requiring a fundamental rebuild.
- Support growth from an initial single-market focus in Bangladesh toward potential future regional expansion.
- Avoid decisions in the current phase that would unnecessarily limit future growth.

## 10. Performance Goals

- Provide customers with a responsive and dependable experience throughout their shopping journey.
- Maintain consistent platform reliability as customer traffic and order volume grow.
- Ensure performance expectations are considered a core quality of the platform, not an afterthought.

Specific performance benchmarks and measurement approaches will be defined in dedicated technical and quality documentation as the platform matures.

## 11. Long-Term Goals

- Grow StackLeo Tech Store from a single-seller B2C platform into a broader, multi-vendor technology marketplace.
- Expand the business model to include B2B sales, serving organizational and bulk customers alongside individual consumers.
- Extend the platform's reach beyond Bangladesh into the wider region, following sustainable, trust-driven growth within the primary market.
- Maintain StackLeo's reputation for reliability and genuine products as the foundation for all future expansion.

## 12. Success Metrics (High-Level)

At a high level, the success of the project's goals will be reflected in:

- Growth in customer trust and repeat engagement with the platform.
- Successful launch and stabilization of the MVP as defined in `project-scope.md`.
- Steady growth in customer base and order volume within the Bangladesh market.
- Successful groundwork laid for future B2B and multi-vendor marketplace capabilities.
- Continued alignment between business outcomes and the goals defined in this document.

Detailed, measurable key performance indicators (KPIs) and targets will be defined in dedicated planning documentation as the project progresses beyond its current planning stage.

## 13. Goal Review Process

Project goals are expected to be reviewed periodically to ensure they remain aligned with business direction, market conditions, and lessons learned as the project progresses. The following principles guide this process:

- Goals should be reviewed whenever significant business, product, or scope decisions are made.
- Any change to the goals defined in this document must be reflected by updating this document and incrementing its version number.
- All goal changes must be recorded in `changelog.md`, including the reasoning behind the change.
- Goals should remain consistent with the boundaries defined in `project-scope.md` and the assumptions and constraints documented in `assumptions.md` and `constraints.md`.
- Reviews should confirm that goals remain realistic and relevant to the project's current stage, whether MVP or a future phase.
